## Game

![](https://www.ageofempires.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Game_Header_Gold.png)

### Stability & Performance

- Improved Trade Cart collision and pathfinding logic, preventing the deterioration of performance to an unstable state under some circumstances.

### ****UI****

- Fixed the Burmese Tech Tree from showing the Padded Archer Armor as Unavailable.

### ****Xbox****

- Fixed several issues causing the game to freeze/crash or reboot the console.

- This includes improvements to memory usage affecting stability on Xbox One models.

### ****PlayStation****

- Fixed several issues causing the game to freeze/crash or reboot the console.

## Gameplay

![](https://www.ageofempires.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Gameplay_Header_Gold.png)

### Fixes

- Inca Monks now use their unique voice lines as intended instead of Female Villager lines.

- Fixed an issue causing Shift + Click to Ungarrison a specific type of Unit from Transport Ships to unload all Units instead of just the selected type.

- Fixed an issue making Rams exceedingly fast if Villagers garrisoned in them were transformed into Flemish Militia with Burgundians' “Flemish Revolution” Unique Technology.

- Fixed an issue where Archer-type units and Fortifications upgraded with Tupi’s “Curare” Unique Technology did not apply poison damage to Cavalry Archer-type units.

- Fixed an issue replacing all drop off buildings with Settlements for every Civilization when starting a game with the Full Tech Tree option enabled.

- Fixed an issue where Elite Longboats would not use the correct Hotkey.

- Fixed an issue where Snakes would not display a dying animation when swimming in water.

- Fixed an issue where creating Sound mods for Female Monks (including South American and *Chronicles* civilizations) was not fully possible without a data mod.

- Fixed an issue where Heavy Demolition Ships had less line of sight than Demolition Ships.

- Fixed an issue where Dragon Ships trained slower than Fire Ships.

## Campaign

![](https://cdn.ageofempires.com/aoe/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Campaign_Header_Gold.webp)

### ****Campaign Missions****

- **El Dorado 5: ‘The Golden King’: **Fixed an issue preventing Ambushers' (Green) Units from evacuating Foundations if the players tried to construct a Building near ambush sites.

- **Lautaro 4: ‘The Elusive Snake’: **Fixed an issue where multiple morale statuses could be displayed for the same allied player.

- **Lautaro 4: ‘The Elusive Snake’:** Players are now correctly awarded with Skirmishers at the beginning of the scenario if they decided to choose the Skirmishers Tribe in Lautaro 1: ‘Gods of Death and Terror’.

- **Lautaro 5: ‘A Taste of Freedom’: **Players can now trade with the Mapuche Tribes (Orange) market.

- **Tamar 4: ‘Tamar the Builder’: **Fixed an issue making Alexios' (Yellow) Cataphracts to attack the Player’s Units despite Alexios being allied to the Player.

## Balance Changes

![](https://www.ageofempires.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Civilization_Balance_Header_Gold.png)

### General

- Champi Scout Adjustments:

- Champi Scout speed reduced from 1.1 → 1.0 (Runner, Warrior and Elite versions unchanged)

- Champi Scout attack reduced from 4 → 3.

- Gain +1 attack vs. Cavalry

- Gain +1 attack vs. Camels

- Gain +2 attack vs. Shock Infantry (+1 → +3)

***Developer Note:** These attack bonuses are only applied to the Champi Scout and not to the Runner, Warrior and Elite. This is targeting the specific interactions between starting scouts in Dark Age.*

- Champi Runner adjustments:

- Champi Runner bonus vs buildings removed (1 → 0)

- Champi Runner upgrade cost increased from 100f 60g → 120f 60g

- Champi Runner upgrade research time increased from 40 seconds to 50 seconds

- Champi Warrior adjustments:

- Champi Warrior upgrade cost increased from 125f 100g → 200f 175g

- Champi-line cost increased from 45f 25g → 50f 25g

- Champi-line Outpost build time changed from 15 → 30/25/20/15 seconds (with Scout/Runner/Warrior/Elite)

- Champi-line Train time increased from 30/30/21/21 seconds → 45/40/26/21 seconds (with Scout/Runner/Warrior/Elite)

### ![](https://cdn.ageofempires.com/aoe/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/mapuche_HD.webp)Mapuche

- Settlement train times adjusted:

- Skirmisher 52 → 56 seconds.

- Elite Skirmisher 33 → 36 seconds.

- Spearman 44 → 47 seconds.

- Pikeman 33 → 36 seconds.

### ![](https://cdn.ageofempires.com/aoe/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/tupi_HD.webp)Tupi

- Curare effect on Blackwood Archers reduced from 3 damage over 15 seconds to 2 damage over 15 seconds (Archer-line unchanged at 5 damage over 15 seconds).

- The dead unit refund bonus for Blackwood Archers now correctly refunds 15% of the total cost (7.5% per each Blackwood Archer).
