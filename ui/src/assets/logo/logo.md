
# aoe2.site Logo Components

This logo combines medieval symbolism with digital motifs to reflect the spirit of AoE2 and the data-driven insights of aoe2.site.

## Components

- **Knight**: Fully armored in dark steel, symbolizing strategy and strength. Helmet plume flowing backward for motion.
- **Shield**: Blue background with a golden lion rampant, evoking heraldic imagery and AoE2's Frankish Paladin.
- **Sword**: Pointed forward, signifying aggression and momentum.
- **Horse**: Leaning upward mid-leap, suggesting a charge into battle or progress.
- **Caparison**: Blue cloth draped over the horse, trimmed in gold.
- **Eddies**: Swirling golden-brown turbulence beneath and overlapping the hindquarters of the horse, inspired by Da Vinci’s studies of hydrodynamics.
- **Binary Code**: 0s and 1s integrated into the eddies, symbolizing data as the force propelling the knight forward.
- **Text**: "aoe2" in a modern serif font, black.
- **Globe Icon**: Simplified Da Vinci-style globe, symbolizing the ".site" and global reach.

## Color Palette

- Midnight Blue
- Gold/Bronze
- Black
- Cream background
